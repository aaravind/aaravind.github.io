ACS Rock Stars 2018

ACS Rock Stars is a recognition program that honours the best and the brightest of ACS India and Global Shared Services (GSS). Established in 2016 to inculcate a culture of meritocracy and appreciation, ACS Rock Stars has come to be one of the most exciting and prestigious awards program for all employees. We’re excited to announce that we’re back with ACS Rock Stars for 2018. And this is your one-stop shop for everything related to the event—from activities and fun contests, to nominations and memories of last years’ winners. Get ready for the extraordinary!

What is ACS Rock Stars ?

ACS Rock Stars is a recognition program for employees of ACS India and GSS. Individuals as well as teams who have delivered exceptional results in the past year are recognised by leaders and peers and rewarded at a grand ceremony amongst colleagues and managers. The ultimate culmination of stellar work and dedication, ACS Rock Stars aims to celebrate the extraordinary in you.

Selection Process

A total of 8 individual and 4 team awards will be given out. i.e., 2 individual recipients and 1 team recipient under each value pillar.

  • Leaders seek nominations from their respective managers/team
  • Leaders review and approve nominations
  • Nominees are announced on the website
  • Final awardees are announced and recognized at the ACS Rockstar event
